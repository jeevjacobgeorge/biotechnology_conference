About Equilibria

Equilibria is a national-level symposium, organized by the Department of Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering (BT & BCE) at Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering (SCTCE),under the banner of Evogen, which encompasses both the staff and student bodies of Biotechnology department.It employs as a pivotal platform for various academic endeavors, including paper presentations, talk sessions, workshops, as well as poster making. With a focus on fostering interdisciplinary dialogue and innovation, Equilibria facilitates the exchange of ideas and expertise among intellectuals, researchers, and students in the fields of biotechnology and biochemical engineering. This dynamic program continues to incentivize and empower participants to push the boundaries of scientific inquiry and technological advancement.

Our Goals

The primary goal of this conference is to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among professionals working in various domains of biotechnology. Through keynote speeches, panel discussions, and interactive sessions, attendees will gain insights into the latest research trends and technological innovations in cross-disciplinary fields of biotechnology .

  • Explore cutting-edge research in various fields
  • Network with leading experts in the field
  • Discuss practical challenges and solutions
  • Gain inspiration for future research endeavors
